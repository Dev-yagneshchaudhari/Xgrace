Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start my project?

We can typically begin a new project within 3-5 business days of signing an agreement. For urgent projects, we offer expedited timelines — just contact us to discuss your needs.

Do you serve clients outside Pune?

Absolutely! While we're based in Pune, we serve clients across India and internationally. Most of our services — web development, software, cloud, digital marketing, training — can be delivered remotely.

What is the cost of your services?

Our pricing varies by service scope and complexity. We offer competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a free consultation and customized quote for your specific requirements.

Do you offer post-project support?

Yes! We offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), ongoing support packages, and dedicated helpdesk services. We believe in long-term relationships and are committed to supporting your business beyond project delivery.
