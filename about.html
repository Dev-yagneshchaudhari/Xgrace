Empowering Business Through Technology

Xrace Technologies is a full-spectrum IT company headquartered in Pune, India. Founded with the vision of making premium technology accessible to every business, we provide a comprehensive suite of IT services under one roof.

From crafting stunning websites and powerful software to setting up cloud infrastructure, delivering IT training, and providing round-the-clock helpdesk support — we handle it all with precision, passion, and professionalism.

Our team of experienced engineers, designers, and IT consultants work together to understand your unique business needs and craft solutions that truly deliver results.

  • Full-spectrum IT services under one roof
  • Experienced team of engineers and consultants
  • Client-centric approach with transparent communication
  • Cutting-edge technology stack
  • Affordable, customized solutions for all business sizes
  • Serving Pune and clients across India

Yogesh Patil — Visionary Leader

Yogesh Patil is the driving force behind Xrace Technologies. With deep expertise in IT infrastructure, software development, and business strategy, he founded Xrace Technologies with a clear vision: to make enterprise-grade IT solutions accessible to businesses of all sizes across India.

Under his leadership, Xrace Technologies has grown from a small IT consultancy into a comprehensive technology company offering 8+ specialized services, serving 50+ clients, and training 500+ students across Pune and beyond.

Yogesh believes in hands-on leadership and is personally involved in key client projects, ensuring that every solution meets Xrace's high standards of quality and innovation.

"Technology should be an enabler, not a barrier. At Xrace, we make sure every client — regardless of size — has access to the tools they need to succeed in the digital era."
